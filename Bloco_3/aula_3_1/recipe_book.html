Aquafaba Mayonnaise

  • Time: less than 10 min
  • Portion: 500 ml
Ingredients Quantity
Aquafaba 6 tbsp (3/8 cup)
Lemon juice 1 tbsp
Vinegar 2 tsp
Salt 1 1/2 tsp
Sugar 1 tsp
Ground mustard 1 tsp
Garlic 2 big cloves
Neutral oil around 1 1/4 cup

Step 1: Blend all ingredients except for the oil in a blender / high speed blender / food processor until you get a frothy an homogenous mix

Step 2: With the blender runing, slowly drizzle the oil until you get a thick consistency (you might need to help the mixing process with a spoon, be carfeul no to hit the blades).

Notes: You could add a 1/2 tsp of turmeric for a light yellow colouring.
You could also replace part of the oil for olive oil for a stronger flavour.
Depending on your aquafaba's concentration, you'll need more or less oil, so be sure to have more available just in case.
